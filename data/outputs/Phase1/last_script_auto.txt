TITLE: Red Planet Tides
GENRE: Science Fiction

SCENE 1 — MARS SURFACE
[TENSE | CONTEMPLATIVE]

MAYA PATEL:
  "This is it. My new home."
  (Maya steps out of her spacecraft, looking out at the Martian landscape with a mix of awe and trepidation)

MISSION CONTROL VOICE:
  "Maya, can you hear us? We're reading you loud and clear."
  (Maya touches her comms device, looking up at the sky)

------------------------------------------------------------

SCENE 2 — HIDDEN OCEAN
[MYSTERIOUS | THRILLING]

MAYA PATEL:
  "What is this place?"
  (Maya shines her flashlight down into the darkness, revealing the glint of water and ice)

DR. LIAM CHEN:
  "Maya, we're reading a massive body of water down there. This could be the discovery of a lifetime."
  (Dr. Chen's voice comes through Maya's comms device, as she looks out at the vast expanse of water)

------------------------------------------------------------

SCENE 3 — OCEAN FLOOR
[ACTION | THRILLING]

MAYA PATEL:
  "Ava, I need you to hack into the submersible's systems and override the safety protocols!"
  (Maya frantically works the controls of the submersible, as Ava's voice comes through the comms device)

AVA MORALES:
  "Maya, I'm trying, but the system is not responding!"
  (Ava's voice is laced with urgency, as Maya's face sets in determination)

------------------------------------------------------------

SCENE 4 — MARS SURFACE
[PEACEFUL | CONTEMPLATIVE]

MAYA PATEL:
  "We did it. We found something incredible."
  (Maya stands at the edge of the Martian landscape, looking out at the sunrise)

DR. LIAM CHEN:
  "Maya, your discovery is going to change the course of human history on Mars."
  (Dr. Chen's voice comes through Maya's comms device, as she smiles, looking out at the landscape)
